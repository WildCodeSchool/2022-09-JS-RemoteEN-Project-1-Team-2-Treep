The climate crisis has been proven to be the main cause of wildfires leading to the loss of 9.3 millions hectares of tree cover globally. If we don’t do anything, wildfires will increase by 30% in 2050. At Treep, we made it our life-long goal to repopulate Mother Earth.

The climate change is already here. If we act now, we can limit warming and reduce the dangers associated with it. Every tiny bit matters.

What we do by 2030 will determine whether we can reduce global warming enough to avoid the worst effects of climate change. We must reduce the emissions drastically and remove some of the carbon from the atmosphere. Luckily, the plants naturally absorb and store carbon. By protecting natural habitats and carefully managing farmland and forests, we can store billions of tons of this " green carbon ".
